About Us

Engineered for India's Most Critical Missions

Excel Ardor Private Limited, incorporated in Hyderabad in 2014, is one of India's leading manufacturers of Telescopic Masts — pneumatic, hydraulic, electromechanical — and Precision Defense Engineering solutions. Holder of a Government of India Patent and ISO 9001:2015 certified, we power India's most critical defense, atomic energy, and aerospace organizations.

From heavy duty masts to light weight masts, hydraulic cylinders and power packs to composite sections and pyro components — every product is designed, manufactured and rigorously tested in-house across our three Hyderabad facilities.

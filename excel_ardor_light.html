Corporate Overview

ENGINEERED FOR INDIA'S FUTURE.

Incorporated in Hyderabad in 2014, Excel Ardor Private Limited is one of India's premier manufacturers of advanced Telescopic Masts and precision defense engineering solutions.

From pneumatic and heavy-duty hydraulic masts to aerospace pyro components, every product is designed, rigorously tested, and manufactured in-house across our three specialized facilities to meet stringent military standards.

Company Profile

ENGINEERING INDIA'S
DEFENSE FUTURE
SINCE 2014.

Excel Ardor Private Limited, incorporated in Hyderabad in 2014, has rapidly emerged as one of India's foremost manufacturers of Telescopic Masts — pneumatic, hydraulic, and electromechanical — alongside high-precision defense aerospace components.

With three strategically located manufacturing facilities in Hyderabad and a team of over 300 combined years of engineering expertise, we serve the nation's most critical institutions with world-class products made entirely in India.

Our patented Internal Hydraulic Telescopic Mast (Patent No. 373182, Govt. of India, 20-year term) is a testament to our commitment to indigenous innovation and engineering excellence.

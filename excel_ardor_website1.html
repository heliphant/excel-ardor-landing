Who We Are

10 Years of Building India's Defence Infrastructure

Incorporated in 2014 in Hyderabad, Excel Ardor Private Limited is a precision engineering company manufacturing cutting-edge telescopic masts, defence sub-assemblies, and special purpose machines for India's most critical defence and aerospace programs.

Precision Mechanical Components

Sub-assemblies for Defence and Aerospace applications manufactured with CNC precision and rigorous quality controls.

Special Purpose Machines

Custom engineered machines for Defence applications, hydraulic systems, scissor lifts, and decontamination equipment.

Automatic Storage Systems

Advanced automated storage and material handling solutions designed for efficiency and reliability.
